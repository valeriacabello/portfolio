Responsive Website

Overview

Rebecca's Rentals wanted a place to host all of the rental listings she has on various platforms. After discussing business goals and gathering content, I created wireframes and mockups to visualize what a website could look like. Then, I hand-coded a mobile-first responsive website consisting of a rental gallery and contact page.



Process

After discussing business needs with the client's advocate and gathering content, I envisoned a website whose main feature would be a rental gallery. The gallery would attract customers with images of the listings and then they would be able to click on the one they like to learn more about it. When designing the wireframes for the website, I wanted to create a design that featured a beautiful listing, an about section, and a testimonials section on the home page, the rental gallery as I envisioned it following a grid format, and an easy to use contact form.
