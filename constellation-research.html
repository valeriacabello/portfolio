Networking Platform

Overview

Constellation is a networking platform that was facing low user engagement and retention. By conducting user research, a heuristic evaluation and usability testing, we were able to identify user goals and motivations as well as usability issues in the website's design to create an action plan for redesign.



Process

Th research consisted of an online survey to discover the psychographics of current and potential users of the site. I created user personas after the end end of the survey to visualize the results that showcase the types of users on Constellation.

Next, I conducted a heuristic evaluation to inspect the website's homepage, navigation and information architecture, trust and credibility, writing and content quality, and page layout and visual design. creating usability The results of the user research and heuristic evaluation helped inform my usbaility testing materials.

I conducted usability testing through virtual meetings with five participants. We used the think-aloud process as participants completed a series of tasks and answered questions in post-task surveys and a post-scenarion questionnaire.

The final deliverable was a slide deck with research findings and recommendations.
